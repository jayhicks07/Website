My Favourite Games

Titanfall 2
Story

SPOILER ALERT

The story in this game is just phenomenal. The fact that you learn to be a pilot with Jack Cooper is just incredible. The game makes you feel for the characters. Sure Titanfall 2 might not have the most memorable, or best video game characters out there; but the fact that I started to care for BT and Jack says a lot about the way that this game's story works. Even just the fact that Titanfall 2 has a singal player campaing is just amazing because Titanfall 1 didn't have one of those. The campaign doesn't take very long to complete but you learn something new in each level.

Titanfall and Apex Legends were developed by Respawn Entertainment that was founded by the people that made Call of Duty when it was good, so MW 1 and 2.

Fortnite: Battle Royale

This game revoloutianized the Battle Royale genre as it included building and editing techniques. This is why the skill gap is so big in this game. Good players can build well, edit fast and accurately, and aim close, medium and long range with all if not most range.

Minecraft

This game is an absolute stunner in terms of video games. It really makes me reminis every time I play or even just think about it. The game has had several updates and the newest update to come out is 1.17, the Cave and Cliffs update. This might be the game changing update which adds the aether and the warden and several new bosses.
